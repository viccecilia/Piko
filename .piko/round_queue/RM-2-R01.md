# Round ID: RM-2-R01

Round Name: Steam Market Connector

本轮目标:

Add a controlled Steam real-market connector contract for hot games and discussion-like player questions.

本轮任务:
- 执行任务:
  - Add Steam connector adapter behind explicit opt-in and configured endpoint.
  - Support normalized hot game records and player question records.
  - Include engagement, reply count, growth, update recency, and Steam rank when present.
  - Keep connector generic enough to support a sanctioned API/proxy endpoint rather than raw website crawling.
- 测试任务:
  - Mock Steam payload and verify normalized output.
  - Test connector refuses to run without opt-in.
  - Test no raw discussion body is retained.
- 协作验收任务:
  - Worker summary must include the Steam endpoint env var and mock record count.

允许修改:

- `packages/collectors/*`
- `packages/discovery/*`
- `packages/shared/config.py`
- `tests/test_discovery_search.py`
- `.piko/summaries/worker_RM-2-R01.md`
- `.piko/round_status.json`

禁止修改:

- Do not scrape Steam web pages directly.
- Do not store full Steam discussion content.
- Do not enable Steam collection by default.

必须运行的验证:

- `python -m pytest tests\test_discovery_search.py -q`

完成定义:

- Steam connector path is mock-tested, opt-in only, bounded, and normalized.
