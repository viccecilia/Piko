# Round ID: RM-4-R02

Round Name: Real Topic To Candidate Pilot

本轮目标:

Take one real-market discovered topic, or a mocked equivalent when live endpoints are absent, and pass it into the discovery-to-article candidate flow.

本轮任务:
- 执行任务:
  - Select the highest safe `publish_candidate` from market rankings.
  - Convert it to an article candidate.
  - Run the candidate workflow in safe draft mode.
  - Write a candidate artifact only if verification and safety fields are preserved.
- 测试任务:
  - Mock real-market topic can become a candidate workflow request.
  - Watchlist/high-risk topics cannot be sent as normal drafts.
  - Candidate artifact remains internal draft, not published.
- 协作验收任务:
  - Worker summary must include selected topic, candidate id, artifact path, and verification status.

允许修改:

- `packages/discovery/*`
- `packages/workflows/*`
- `artifacts/candidate_drafts/*`
- `tests/test_discovery_search.py`
- `.piko/summaries/worker_RM-4-R02.md`
- `.piko/round_status.json`

禁止修改:

- Do not publish candidate output.
- Do not deploy public pages.
- Do not weaken candidate verification to make a pilot pass.

必须运行的验证:

- `python -m pytest tests\test_discovery_search.py -q`
- `python -m packages.workflows.article_pipeline`

完成定义:

- One real-market-style topic can flow into candidate workflow safely, or is blocked with a clear reason.

输出格式:
- 修改了什么

- 协作验收结果
