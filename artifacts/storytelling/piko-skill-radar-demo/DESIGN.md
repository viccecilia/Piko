# Style Prompt

Technical knowledge short video with a dark interface canvas, bright white Chinese typography, cyan and rose accents, floating UI cards, subtle screenshot background, and crisp motion. It should feel like a serious AI-builder explanation, not a playful ad.

# Colors

- Background: `#0b0f14`
- Panel: `#111923`
- Line: `#243244`
- Text: `#f7fafc`
- Muted: `#cbd5e1`
- Cyan Accent: `#64d2ff`
- Rose Accent: `#ff4f8b`

# Typography

- Inter
- Noto Sans SC

# What NOT to Do

- Do not use stock-like decorative blobs.
- Do not use tiny text.
- Do not make it look like a landing page.
- Do not overload one scene with full paragraphs.
- Do not imitate a specific creator's voice or identity.
